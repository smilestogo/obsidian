## Page 11 — Modern, Proprietary Technology Platform

GTN's platform delivers reliability and performance while ensuring the security demanded in financial services.

### Platform Highlights
- Proprietary, **institutional-grade platform** supports multi-asset trading globally
- **Component-based, multi-tenant architecture** allows different asset classes and execution strategies to be added with relative ease and delivers more efficient order processing
- **Flexible API ecosystem** drives quick time to value
- **Rich integration capabilities** support both modern and legacy systems and allow GTN to onboard new clients in a seamless, low-effort manner
- **Modular, front-end applications** enable clients to trade anytime anywhere
- **Customizable white-label** web, mobile and desktop offerings, providing customer branding to end-users
- For fintechs building **transactional ecosystems**
- **Scalability and resilience** through strategic partnerships
- GTN has long-standing relationships with best-in-class infrastructure providers, allowing for rapid global deployment and dynamic scaling
- **Dedicated team of in-house technology experts** providing full support
- Experienced team of domain experts handling account, trading or post-trade matters, regionally located

### Architecture Stack
- **GTN Order Routing** — FIX Connectivity, OTC Plug-ins
- **GTN OMS** — Order management
- **GTN Admin / Dealer Terminal** — Connect back-office and deal staff to OMS
- **Exchange & Brokers Network**
- **GTN Price Backbone** — Global Market Data
- **GTN Pro, Web & Mobile** — Trade using co-branded web and mobile apps
- **API / Widget Framework** — Content Contribution Platform
- **GTN Invest, AMT, AMA, BO** — Manage end-client operations and investment performance without disclosing details (Digital Wealth)
- **OTC Data / Content Management**
- **GTN Invest Web / Mobile** — Clients can easily view investment performance

## Page 14 — Three Distinct Solutions Allow GTN to Serve a Broad Spectrum of Clients

### Solution 1 — Core B2B / B2B2C Global Trading Platform
**Target Clients:** Brokers, Banks, Asset Managers, Wealth Managers
- Single point of integration, counterparty relationship and execution network
- Sophisticated order & execution management system and a middle/back-office system for account management and reporting
- Co-branded web and mobile applications
- Fractional trading capabilities across asset classes

### Solution 2 — Embedded Finance Solution
**Target Clients:** Digital Wallets / Payments, Neo Banks / Brokers, Consumer Super-Apps
- Frictionless global investing and trading experiences
- Suite of APIs available piecemeal
- Modular design, with multiple integration pathways (open APIs, FIX connectivity, customer front-ends and investment office offering)
- Delivering key drivers of financial inclusion, including micro investing and micro portfolio functionalities

### Solution 3 — End-to-End Digital Wealth Management Solution
**Target Clients:** Brokers & Asset Managers, Wealth Managers, Banks & Neo Banks and Wealth Techs
- Automate the portfolio and investment management process
- Unified infrastructure, seamlessly connected with GTN Trade & GTN Invest via APIs and widgets (cloud)
- Rationalized onboarding process, portfolio management, portfolio monitoring, portfolio rebalancing and administration
