## Page 2 — About GTN

We are a fintech pioneer with decades of success, holding broker-dealer and capital markets services licenses in multiple jurisdictions through subsidiaries. Our commitment is to empower brokers, banks, asset managers, and fintechs with scalable and innovative investment and trading solutions that enable access to a comprehensive network of global markets and multiple asset classes, making investment and trading accessible to all.

With a global reach spanning over **80 countries**, we offer execution and custody services across a broad spectrum of asset classes, ensuring a truly international presence.

Our investment and trading solutions offer seamless integration with the existing services of regulated financial firms and fintechs via our co-branded front-ends, **GTN Trade** and **GTN Invest**, FIX connectivity and versatile API suite. Inspired by embedded finance, our API suite provides the adaptability to develop bespoke trading/investment applications or incorporate our features into existing platforms, enhancing their value proposition.

Our comprehensive global trading ecosystem includes best-in-class execution, custody, and post-trade solutions.

- Based on the **"wholesaler" model**, we operate within a B2B and B2B2C framework, effectively avoiding conflicts of interest with B2C operations and ensuring effortless collaboration with our partners.
- Backed by strategic investors like the **IFC** (a member of the World Bank Group), and **SBI Ventures Singapore Pte. Ltd.** (a group company of SBI Holdings, Inc., one of the largest financial services firms listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange).
- We bring together a diverse team of over **350 talented individuals** spread across Dubai, Singapore, South Africa, Sri Lanka, the UK, and the US, united by a shared passion and purpose: empowering clients and transforming the accessibility to investment and trading opportunities for all.

## Page 6 — Best Execution / Asset Coverage

GTN's coverage encompasses **Equity, ETFs, Options, Fixed Income, Mutual Funds, Structured Products, FX, CFDs**, and more, catering to a broad range of investment needs.

### Best Execution: Conflict-Free
GTN abides strictly by the perimeters of best execution. Our brokerage is an **agency-only model** by which we remain market neutral. To avoid any conflicts of interest, GTN is **not a market maker**. When we execute an order, we take all reasonable steps to obtain the best possible result using a range of different factors such as price, speed of execution and costs associated with trading in that venue.

### Equity, Fixed Income, Funds, CFDs, FX
Our Order Desk provides an advanced suite of online reporting, resulting in a comprehensive front-to-back solution.

- Electronic market access tools / Algorithmic trading
